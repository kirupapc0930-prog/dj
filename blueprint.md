# **App Name**: Dubai Tronics

## Core Features:

- Product Display: Showcase electronics with images, names, ratings, and prices.
- Product Search: Implement a search bar with suggestions for easy product discovery.
- User Authentication: Allow users to create profiles, log in, and manage their information.
- Shopping Cart: Enable users to add, remove, and adjust quantities of products in a cart.
- Wishlist: Let users save products for later purchase in a wishlist.
- Order Tracking: Provide a system for users to track the status of their orders.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: The app should feature a tool that analyzes user browsing history and purchase patterns to provide personalized product recommendations, enhancing the shopping experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#FFD700) for a premium and luxurious feel, reflecting Dubai's opulence.
- Background color: White (#F8F8FF) to provide a clean, modern canvas that complements the gold.
- Accent color: Royal Blue (#4169E1) to highlight key elements and CTAs with a touch of sophistication.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif providing a contemporary, precise feel, as requested by the user. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent categories and features.
- Follow an Amazon-like layout for familiarity, with a prominent search bar, category menu, and product grids.
- Subtle animations for banners and product transitions to enhance user engagement.
